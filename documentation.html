>>> HOW TO USE PIXELFLUX <<<

Learn every tool in PixelFlux step by step. Screenshots highlight each numbered feature below.

Main Editor Tools (1–13)

>>> TOOLS EXPLAINED <<<

  1. 1. Edit/View toggle – Switch between editing your canvas or viewing it without tools in the way.
  2. 2. Undo – Revert your last action instantly.
  3. 3. Redo – Restore the step you just undid.
  4. 4. Export options – Save your art as PNG, GIF, or PixelFlux’s native .pxlflux format.
  5. 5. Full Screen option – Focus completely on your work without distractions.
  6. 6. Set Bg color – Change background color for better contrast.
  7. 7. Color palettes – Switch or load palettes to speed up your workflow.
  8. 8. Selection tool – Select, move, or copy specific parts of your canvas.
  9. 9. Import pixels – Import an image or another project into your canvas.
  10. 10. Symmetry tool – Draw mirrored lines or patterns automatically.
  11. 11. Fill tool – Fill an area with a single color in one tap.
  12. 12. Eraser tool – Remove pixels with precision.
  13. 13. Add color to palette – Store your current color in the palette for reuse.
Animation Tools (14–20)

>>> ANIMATION TOOLS <<<

  1. 14. Play/Stop – Preview your animation in real-time.
  2. 15. Toggle previous onion skin – See a ghosted version of the previous frame for smooth transitions.
  3. 16. Toggle next onion skin – Preview the upcoming frame over your canvas.
  4. 17. FPS count – Adjust animation speed in frames per second.
  5. 18. Duplicate frame – Copy your current frame to speed up animation creation.
  6. 19. Create new blank frame – Add a fresh empty frame for new motion.
  7. 20. Timeline – Manage, reorder, or delete frames easily.
